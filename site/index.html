The loop

From a sentence to a real PR.

Three steps. No mockups, no copy-paste, no re-implementation by hand.

  1. 01

    Talk

    Open the side panel on any page and describe the change in plain English, “make the hero full-bleed, tighten the nav, CTA orange.”

  2. 02

    See

    The agent mutates the real DOM and CSS instantly. You watch it happen, react, and refine in conversation until the page is exactly right.

  3. 03

    Ship

    Click Ship. A structured changeset, selectors, styles, intent, goes over MCP to your dev agent, which edits the source and opens a tested, reviewable PR.
